Wilted Flowers

Wilted flowers are a lovely way to celebrate a special occasion. This quick recipe can be prepared in less than a week and only requires a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients
  • Bacteria (Pseudomonas aeruginosa if available)
  • Water
  • Ribbon or String
  • Sunlight

Recipe Steps

  1. Add excessive water to the vase that contains the flowers.
  2. Mix bacteria into the water until the water becomes moderately turbid.
  3. Allow the flowers to incubate in the bacteria water for several days.
  4. When the flowers begin to look wilted, remove all of the water.
  5. Place the vase in sunlight until the wilting process completes
  6. Remove the wilted flowers from the vase
  7. Tie a fancy bow around your wilted flowers with your choice of ribbon or string.
